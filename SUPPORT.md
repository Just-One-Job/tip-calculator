## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I calculate a tip?

1. Enter your bill amount using the numeric keypad
2. Select a tip percentage (or tap "Custom" for a different amount)
3. Optionally, use the +/- buttons to split the bill between multiple people
4. View your results in the summary section

### Can I use decimal tip percentages?

Yes! Tap "Custom" and enter a decimal percentage like 17.5%.

### How do I clear the bill amount?

Long-press the backspace button (⌫) to clear the entire amount, or tap backspace repeatedly to delete digits one by one.

### Does the app remember my preferences?

Yes! The app remembers your last used tip percentage and split count. These are stored locally on your device and never shared.

### How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Tap the theme toggle in the top-right corner of the screen.

### Is my data private?

Absolutely! Tip Calculator does not collect, track, or transmit any data. All calculations happen on your device, and your preferences are stored locally only.

### The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

1. Try closing and reopening the app
2. Make sure you're running the latest version
3. If the issue persists, email us at david@justonejobapps.com with:
   - Your device model and OS version
   - A description of the issue
   - Steps to reproduce the problem

### Can I suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear your ideas! However, Tip Calculator follows the "Just One Job" philosophy—one screen, one purpose. We're focused on keeping it simple and fast. Email us your suggestions, and we'll consider them if they align with this philosophy.

### Is Tip Calculator free?

Yes! Tip Calculator is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no hidden costs.
