# App Store Listing Information

## App Name
**Tip Calculator – Just One Job**

## Subtitle
One-screen, no-ads tip calculator

## Description

Tip Calculator is a super clean, one-screen app that does exactly one thing: calculates tips quickly and accurately.

### Features

**Simple & Fast**
- One screen, zero distractions
- Instant calculations as you type
- Large, thumb-friendly buttons
- Dark mode support

**Smart & Helpful**
- Quick tip presets or custom percentage
- Split bill between multiple people
- Remembers your preferences
- Haptic feedback for better interaction

**Privacy First**
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics
- All calculations happen locally on your device
- No data collection whatsoever

**Just One Job**
Built with the "Just One Job" philosophy—one app, one purpose, no BS. No settings screens, no upsells, no fluff. Just a calculator that works.

Perfect for restaurants, bars, cafes, and anywhere you need to calculate tips quickly.
